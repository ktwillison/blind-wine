Final Project - The Wine Finder

The Wine Finder was created by fellow oenophiles Ben Khoo and Kate Willison, so that their blind-tasting companions might have an easier time identifying varietals based on their unique properties. The visualization leverages crossfilter.js, DC.js and Leaflet, as well as an adaptation of Mike Bostock's Sunburst visualization.

Digging Into the Iris Dataset

This visualization dives into the features available in the classic Iris dataset, visualizing the relationship between each as the user filters the data in real time. The libraries used are crossfilter.js and DC.js.

Donald Trump's Media Domination

This visualization traces the rise and rise of Donald Trump's media coverage, leveraging data from the New York Times Article Search API. The library used was C3.js.


Doughnut toppings and fillings

This visualization shows all of the fabulous combinations of doughnuts that exist somewhere out there. The library used was plain old D3.js.
